Our App

different subparts which will then have different activities.

About Us

Our Aim is to raise awareness to the society to conserve and save water as it is a very vital resource and can be exhausted by overuse and unwise use. We in this age don't know the importance of fresh pure water, in the old generation people would spend days finding fresh and pure water and they had no way to test if it had germs or not, they would fall ill and in the worst cases they would die. Our app helps people understand the importance of water and ways to purify it or tricks to save water. This will enable young kids to know how to purify water and do many more science experiments which help. With this app there will be many lives saved and we can look into a better future for our young ones.

Water Facts!

  • 68.7% of the fresh water on Earth is trapped in glaciers.
  • About 6,800 gallons of water is required to grow a day's food for a family of four.
  • 70% of the human brain is water.
  • The freezing point of water lowers as the amount of salt dissolved in at increases. With average levels of salt, seawater freezes at -2 °C (28.4 °F).
